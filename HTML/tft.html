Teamfight Tactics


Teamfight Tactics (or TFT) is an auto battler game developed and published by Riot Games.

The goal is to compete online against seven other opponents by building a team using units and items to be the last one standing.

Unit rarity is separated into category's based on cost, ranging from 1 to 5 gold per unit, higher rarity typically featuring more powerful abilities.
